Devon Hase, MAT, MFA, began meditation training in 2001. After spending a decade as a classroom teacher of high school and community college students, she entered a cumulative two-year period of retreat guided by Joseph Goldstein, Eugene Cash, and her Tibetan Buddhist teachers, Lama Pema and Lama Yeshe. Currently, Devon serves on retreat teams at IMS and Spirit Rock, and leads trainings in the US and Europe with her husband, Craig. She is the co-author of the book, How Not To Be A Hot Mess: A Survival Guide for Modern Life. She loves long retreat and is passionate about creative ways to bring depth practice into our everyday lives. She lives in Ashland, Oregon and is a participant in IMS's 2017-2021 Teacher Training Program.
